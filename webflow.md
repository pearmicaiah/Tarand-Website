> **Purpose of this document**

## 1. CORE PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

- Video-first, not text-first
- Sector-led architecture, not brand chaos
- Every subsidiary must clearly answer:
  - What it does
  - Which sector it belongs to
  - Why it exists in the group

- Designed to scale as new companies are added

## 5. INNOVATION LAYER (STATE OF THE ART)

### 5.1 THE "LIVING ORGANISM" WEBGL HOME
- **Concept:** A slow-rotating 3D cluster where each node represents a subsidiary.
- **Interaction:** Hovering shifts the ambient background to the sector’s color palette.
- **Goal:** Visually proves Tarand is a complex, connected, living ecosystem.

### 5.2 "PULSE OF THE GROUP" (LIVE DATA)
- **Concept:** "Bloomberg Terminal for Industry" ticker showing operational metrics.
- **Metrics:** "kWh Generated," "Tons Produced," "Gold Ounces Extracted."
- **Style:** Minimalist, monospaced typography with kinetic counters.

### 5.3 CINEMATIC SCROLLYTELLING
- **Concept:** Fluid "morphing" transitions between sectors instead of hard cuts.
- **Tech:** WebGL/GSAP Flip transitions.
- **Effect:** Continuous documentary-style flow from Energy (dark/industrial) to Agriculture (bright/green).

### 5.4 BENTO GRID NAVIGATION
- **Concept:** Full-screen overlay of uneven, rounded tiles for sector navigation.
- **Content:** Mix of video loops, static numbers, and images.
- **Interaction:** "Magnetic" cursor effects and tactile feel.

### 5.5 SOUNDSCAPES (SUBTLE AUDIO)
- **Concept:** Low-volume ambient sound changing by sector (Mining = deep earth, Ag = wind).
- **Control:** Default "Off", user-enabled for immersion.
