Grid-Scale Hybrid Power for Remote Mining

How Flora Solar reduced diesel dependency by 40% for Continental Treasures Mining.

Client
Continental Treasures
Location
Kadoma, Zimbabwe

The Challenge

Running a tier-1 gold mine requires uninterrupted baseload power. With national grid instability and rising diesel costs, Continental Treasures was facing an operational expenditure crisis.

The directive was clear: Reduce OPEX without compromising a single minute of uptime.

The Solution

01

Load Analysis

Deployed smart meters to map peak load consumption vs. baseload requirements over a 30-day period.

02

Hybrid Integration

Installed a 2.5MW solar tracking farm integrated directly with existing diesel generators via a master controller.

03

Smart Optimization

Automated logic prioritizes solar > battery > grid > diesel, ensuring cheapest power source is always used first.
